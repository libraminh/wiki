Dear Cybersecurity Enthusiast,

If you have not sign up, this is a follow up email to the earlier invitation to ST Engineering’s inaugural Cybersecurity Summit 2018 on Friday, 9 Nov 2018. The theme for the seminar is “ Taking Back the Initiative ”.

Cyber-attacks have become more frequent. Consequences of an attack can be severe; whether it is a data compromise or disruption to critical infrastructure. Too often are we finding ourselves reacting to cyber-incidents. We are constantly being put on the back-foot by cyber-attackers and picking up pieces in the aftermath. This Summit brings together eminent experts from across the globe to share why, how and what we can do to stop being passive in the face of cyber-threats. We have invited speakers with experience in designing and managing cyber security policies and systems as well as cyber-practitioners. They will share their perspective on what leaders like yourself can do to take back the initiative and be proactive in taking on the cybersecurity challenges in an increasingly challenging environment.

The Summit will also be an opportunity for you to join other leaders and top management from government agencies, statutory boards, CIIs and commercial enterprises to discuss the future of cybersecurity.

It’s our pleasure and honour to invite you to join us at the inaugural Summit.

We look forward to seeing you at the ST Engineering Cybersecurity Summit 2018!

Warmest Regards,

ST Engineering Electronics
